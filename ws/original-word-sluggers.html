Welcome Original Word Sluggers!

That should get you up and running in the new game. Enjoy!

If you'd like to connect your new account with your original Word Slug player account follow the instructions below:

In the original Word Slug app
STEP 1

Start a new practice game with RoboSlug.

Play your first word and write it down.

We'll ask you for this later as proof of who you are.

Download and install Word Slug 2
STEP 2

If you've already downloaded and installed the new app you can skip ahead to step 3.

Click the appropriate button below on the device you wish to install Word Slug 2 and follow the instructions.

Or you can search for Word Slug 2 in the app store on your device. You may need to include double quotes when searching to find an exact match. For example, "Word Slug 2"

In Word Slug 2
STEP 3

Once you've opened Word Slug 2 and created an account, from the main screen click the gear icon in the upper left.

On the Options screen the bottom tab contains Account related options. Find the 'Support' button and click it. That will pop open your email client with your new user name already in the email. If you haven't updated the name it will look something like 'WS123456789000'. In the email let us know your old user name and that first word you wrote down above in step 1. Send us that email and we'll connect your new and old accounts. This may take us a couple of days. Be patient. We'll contact you if we need additional information. Note that we'll never ask you for your password.
